1. What is a Green's Function?

G(x, y) = min(x,y) · (1 − max(x,y))  →  u(x) = ∫₀¹ G(x,y) f(y) dy
G(x, y) — the full kernel
hover left/right to pick y₀
G(x, y₀) — response to a point source at y₀ = 0.50
G(x, y₀) is the solution u when f = δ(x − y₀): a single point source at y₀. The tent shape reflects how the source influences nearby points most. Any forcing f is solved by summing these tents — weighted by f(y).
